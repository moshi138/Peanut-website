Luis Díaz is a Colombian professional footballer who plays as a left winger for Liverpool FC and the Colombia national team. Born on January 13, 1997, in Barrancas, Colombia, he started his career at Barranquilla FC before moving to Atlético Junior, where he won the Colombian league, Copa Colombia, and Superliga. In 2019, Díaz joined FC Porto for €7 million, winning two Primeira Liga titles, a Portuguese Cup, and a Super Cup. His impressive performances earned him a move to Liverpool in 2022 for €45 million, where he won the FA Cup and EFL Cup in his first season. Internationally, Díaz has been a key player for Colombia, earning over 50 caps and winning the Golden Boot at Copa América 2021. He helped Colombia finish third in 2021 and runners-up in 2024
